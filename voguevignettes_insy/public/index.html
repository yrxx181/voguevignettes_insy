RUNWAY

What are runway walks?

Runway walks or also known as fashion shows are an event put on by a fashion designer to showcase their upcoming line of clothing and/or accessories during a fashion week. Fashion shows debut every season, particularly the spring/summer and fall/winter seasons. This is where designers seek to promote their new fashions.

Famous fashion shows include:

Alexander McQueen Spring 1999 show, the Versace Autumn/Winter 1991 show, the Chanel Spring 1994 show, and the Gucci Fall/Winter 1995 show.
